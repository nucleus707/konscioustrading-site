Privacy Policy for KonsciousTrading

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

KonsciousTrading ("we", "us", or "our") operates the KonsciousTrading browser extension. This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our extension.

1. Data Collection and Use

We prioritize your privacy. We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide the features of the extension.

  • Authentication (Identity): We use Google Authentication (OAuth2) to allow you to sign in. We only access your email address and basic profile information to create your user account and sync your settings across devices. We do not share this data with third parties.
  • Trading Data: The extension reads your trading balance and trade outcomes directly from the Pocket Option web page to enforce your local risk settings (e.g., stopping trading after X losses). This data is processed locally on your device or synced to your own private user profile for the sole purpose of maintaining your trade counts. We do not sell or analyze your trading strategies.
  • Local Storage: We use your browser's local storage to save your preferences, such as "Max Trades per Day" or "Work Duration."

2. Data Security

Your data is protected using industry-standard encryption via Supabase (our database provider) and Google Authentication. Access to your settings is restricted to your authenticated account.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to function:

  • Google OAuth: For secure user authentication.
  • Supabase: For storing your user configuration settings in the cloud.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. To request deletion, please contact us at the email below.
